Understanding Evaluation Status
Not Started

Staff member has not begun the evaluation process. No targets or self-assessments submitted.

Draft/In Progress

Evaluation in progress but not yet submitted for review.

Submitted

Evaluation submitted and awaiting supervisor review.

Reviewed

Supervisor has reviewed and provided feedback.

Completed/Finalized

Evaluation completed and finalized in the system.

Evaluated (Supervisors)

Supervisor has been evaluated by VC/HR. This is the final status for supervisors.

Viewing Reports

• Regular Staff: Click "View" to see detailed evaluation report
• Supervisors: Click "View" to see VC evaluation report (Available for Submitted, Reviewed, Completed, Finalized, and Evaluated statuses)
• PDF Download: Available for all completed evaluations
• In Progress: Can still view partial evaluations
